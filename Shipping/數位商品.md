
## 商店配送方式

- ShopShippingType
- TypeName

既有資料
| ShopShippingType_ShopId | ShopShippingType_TypeName | ShopShippingType_Id | ShopShippingType_SupplierId |
|-------------------------|---------------------------|---------------------|----------------------------|
| 39 | 宅配 | 336 | 36 |
| 39 | 付款後門市自取 | 355 | 36 |

```sql

use WebStoreDB
--門市企業測試店

declare @shopId BIGINT = 39,
		@SupplierId INT;　-- 36

SELECT @SupplierId = Shop_SupplierId
FROM Shop(NOLOCK)
WHERE Shop_ValidFlag = 1
AND Shop_Id = @shopId;

SELECT ShopShippingType_TypeName,*
FROM ShopShippingType(NOLOCK)
WHERE ShopShippingType_ValidFlag = 1
AND ShopShippingType_ShopId = @shopId
```
